
  # Privacy Policy — WordHowler

  **Last updated: June 2026**
  
  WordHowler ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated as an independent
  project. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use
  it, and your rights around it. We have kept it short and in plain
  English on purpose.

  ---

  ## What We Collect

  ### What you provide
  - **Username** — the display name you choose when you first open the
    app. This is visible to other players on the leaderboard.
  
  ### What we generate automatically
  - **Device ID** — a random identifier created on your device the first
    time you launch the app. It is never linked to your name, email, or
    any account outside WordHowler. It is used solely to remember you
    between sessions and to submit your scores.
  
  ### What we record during gameplay
  - **Words you submit** and the **scores** you earn each round.
  - **Round results** — your rank, best word, and bonus breakdowns per
    round.
  - **Reactions** you send or receive (emoji code + timestamp only).

  ### What we do NOT collect
  - Your real name, email address, or phone number.
  - Your location.
  - Your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  - Payment or financial information of any kind.
  - Any data from other apps on your device.

  ---

  ## How We Use Your Information

  | Data | Purpose |
  |------|---------|
  | Username | Display on leaderboard and in reactions |
  | Device ID | Authenticate you without a login/password |
  | Words & scores | Calculate round results and all-time leaderboard |
  | Reactions | Deliver emoji reactions to the recipient in real time |

  We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties
  for advertising, analytics, or any other commercial purpose.

  ---

  ## Data Storage

  Your data is stored on servers hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in
  the United States. Round results are retained to power the all-time
  leaderboard. Raw gameplay logs may be retained for up to 90 days for
  debugging purposes and then deleted.

  ---

  ## Children's Privacy

  WordHowler does not knowingly collect personal information from children
  under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal information
  to play — only a chosen username and an auto-generated device ID. If
  you believe a child has submitted personal information through the app,
  please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
  
  ---

  ## Advertising

  The current version of WordHowler contains no advertisements and no
  advertising SDKs. If advertising is introduced in a future version,
  this policy will be updated before that version is released and users
  will be notified through an app update.
  
  ---
  ## Your Rights
  
  You can:
  - **Change your username** at any time from the Settings screen inside
    the app.
  - **Delete all your data** by uninstalling the app. Because your account
    is tied to your device ID (not an email or external account),
    uninstalling permanently removes your ability to recover that account.
    If you would like your stored scores and username explicitly removed
    from our servers, contact us at the email below.
    
  ---
  
  ## Third-Party Services
  
  WordHowler uses the following infrastructure services to operate. These
  providers process data only as directed by us and are bound by their
  own privacy commitments.
  
  - **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** — cloud hosting, database, and content
    delivery. [AWS Privacy Policy](https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/)
    
  No analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel), crash-reporting SDKs
  (e.g. Sentry, Crashlytics), or social login SDKs are included in the
  current version of the app.
